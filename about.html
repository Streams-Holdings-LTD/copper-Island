🏝️ Our Story

Building the Future of
3D Printing Commerce

Copper Island Collectables connects passionate Canadian 3D printing brands with makers, hobbyists, educators, and resellers — all on one transparent wholesale platform.

Our Mission

Empowering Every Maker

We started Copper Island Collectables because we were tired of sourcing 3D printing supplies from a dozen different websites, waiting weeks for international shipping, and never knowing if a brand was trustworthy.

So we built the platform we always wanted — a curated wholesale marketplace where every brand is vetted, every product is authentic, and every maker gets wholesale pricing from day one.

Based in Canada, built for Canada's growing maker community. Whether you run a makerspace, a tabletop gaming shop, an online store, or just love printing at home — this platform is for you.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

🔍

Curation

Every brand on our platform is manually reviewed. We verify quality, test products, and only onboard brands we'd buy from ourselves.

💰

Fair Pricing

Wholesale pricing for everyone — no secret tiers, no minimum spend surprises. Small makers deserve the same deals as big buyers.

🇨🇦

Canadian First

We prioritise Canadian brands, Canadian shipping, and Canadian payment terms. Supporting local is built into our DNA.

🤝

Community

This platform grows when makers share, review, and connect. We invest in community features, not just commerce.

🌱

Sustainability

We favour brands with eco-certifications, biodegradable materials, and responsible manufacturing. Making should last.

🔒

Transparency

Every product has honest specs, real reviews, and real wholesale prices. No hidden fees. No false markdowns.
